Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Unirad Tele Radiology solutions, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our clients' and their patients' information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard personal and health-related data in compliance with international and national standards.

1. Data Collection

We collect information necessary for providing high-quality Tele radiology services. This includes:

  • Patient identification details provided by healthcare facilities.
  • Diagnostic images and clinical information relevant to the radiology studies.
  • Contact information of healthcare professionals and institutional representatives.

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used exclusively for:

  • Generating diagnostic radiology reports.
  • Facilitating communication between our radiologists and referring physicians.
  • Internal quality assurance and clinical peer review processes.
  • Billing and administrative purposes related to the services provided.

3. Data Security & Confidentiality

We employ advanced technical and organizational measures to ensure data integrity and confidentiality:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmissions.
  • Secure, multi-factor authenticated access to our cloud-based PACS/RIS infrastructure.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Strict non-disclosure agreements with all personnel and authorized sub-contractors.

4. Legal & Regulatory Compliance

Unirad Tele Radiology solutions adheres to the following frameworks:

  • Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) standards for data privacy and security.
  • Information Technology Act, 2000 (India) and its subsequent amendments and rules.
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles where applicable.
