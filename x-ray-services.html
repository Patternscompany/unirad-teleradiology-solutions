X-Ray Services

Precision X-Ray & Digital Radiography

X-ray remains the most frequently utilized imaging modality in clinical medicine. At Unirad Tele Radiology solutions, we provide robust interpretation services for all types of plain film and digital radiography studies.

Our streamlined workflow allows for the rapid processing of high volumes of X-ray studies, making us an ideal partner for urgent care centers and primary care clinics. We ensure that every 'stat' study is prioritized for immediate review.

Reporting Excellence

Our team of subspecialized radiologists ensure that you receive the highest quality of care. We are dedicated to providing precise diagnostic insights for complex cases.

We offer a range of reporting solutions tailored to your facility's needs, from emergency nighthawk coverage to routine subspecialty interpretation.

  • Rapid Turnaround Times for Urgent Studies
  • Board-Certified Subspecialty Radiologists
  • 24/7/365 Clinical Support

Committed to Diagnostic Precision

Unirad Tele Radiology solutions is built on a foundation of medical excellence and technological innovation. We understand that behind every image is a patient waiting for answers, and we strive to provide the most accurate and timely results possible.

We provide a secure, HIPAA-compliant gateway for image transfer. Our technical team works with your staff to establish a seamless DICOM connection from your scanner or PACS.
